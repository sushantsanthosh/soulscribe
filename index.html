How to Use

Welcome to SoulScribe! Our interface is quick and simple to use. To start, go to the ‘Journal’ section, and answer the given prompt. Once you are satisfied with your answer, click ‘Save Answer’ to save your journal entry. To see your previous answers to the prompts, go to the ‘Calendar’ tab, and click on the day of which response you are looking for. You can also toggle through the months using the arrows next to the month name. The days for which the boxes are filled in, indicate days where you have put in a journal entry. This will help you track how consistent you are with your responses. Also, be sure to check out the daily regenerating quotes in the ‘Calendar’ tab!

About Us

At SoulScribe, we're more than just a team—we're survivors and warriors of our own mental health battles. Throughout high school, the three of us witnessed firsthand the pain and struggles that mental health issues can cause, not only in ourselves but in our friends and loved ones. We know what it's like to feel isolated, misunderstood, and overwhelmed. That's why we couldn't stand by any longer. We felt a responsibility to take action, to turn our shared experiences into something positive and supportive. This website is our heartfelt effort to reach out to those who are struggling, to let you know that you're not alone, and to provide the resources and support that we wish we had. Together, we can face these challenges and find strength in our shared journey.
